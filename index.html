  • 02 What are Industrial Projects ?

    IR&P council has one of its primary objectives to give students industrial exposure in the running semester. IR&P council contacts various industry giants and start-ups and brings the live industry problem statements for students to work. It gives students excellent exposure to working with industry professionals under the mentorship of IITGN faculties.

  • 03What are Institute Building Projects?

    Institute Building project(IBP) is an initiative of IIT GN. IR&P Council handles these projects. These projects aim to increase student participation in institute work and give them monetary incentives for their participation. Students build good connections with faculties and other students while working on these projects. These projects also provide them an exposure to solving real-life problems.
